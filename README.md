# Mixtape

A music player for your own files. Import your tracks, organize them into a library, and play them on Mac and iPhone. Your library stays in sync across devices.

## What it does

- Import audio files from your computer or phone
- Browse by songs, albums, artists, and playlists
- Build and edit playlists
- Favourite tracks
- Play, queue, and shuffle, with a full-screen now playing view
- Download tracks for offline listening and export them back out
- Sync your library between devices

## Requirements

- macOS 14 or later
- iOS 17 or later

## Installing (Mac)

Download the latest `.dmg` from the [Releases](https://github.com/mikec-1/Mixtape/releases) page, open it, and drag Mixtape into your Applications folder.

On the first launch, right-click the app and choose Open. After that it opens normally, and the app keeps itself up to date.

## Building from source

Open `Mixtape.xcodeproj` in Xcode and build the Mixtape scheme. Dependencies are handled through Swift Package Manager.

## Version

Version 1.0. This is the public build, kept intentionally simple.
